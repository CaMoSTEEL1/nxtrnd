# .impeccable.md — Design Context

## Design Context

### Users
**Agency operators** managing UGC content production for multiple sports brand clients (Nike, Lululemon, supplement companies, athleisure startups). They're running content pipelines, not just one-off projects. Their context: busy, results-driven, accountable to clients. The job to be done is generating polished, TikTok/Reels-ready AI influencer videos at scale without the overhead of traditional influencer hiring. The interface must feel **organized, scalable, and trustworthy** — both when they're heads-down in workflow and when they're screen-sharing with a client.

### Brand Personality
- **Three words**: Capable, Polished, Scalable
- **Tone**: Professional with creative confidence — not corporate stiff, not startup chaotic. The tool does serious AI work but shouldn't feel intimidating.
- **Emotional goal**: Operators should feel *in control of something powerful*. Confidence when generating content, clarity when reviewing outputs, satisfaction when delivering to clients.

### Aesthetic Direction
- **Visual tone**: Clean premium SaaS — think Stripe, Notion, or Linear light. Light backgrounds (`gray-50`/`white`), refined card surfaces, subtle shadows, generous whitespace.
- **Primary palette**: Deep indigo/violet — authoritative, modern, and distinct from typical sports-red energy. Signals intelligence and craft.
- **Theme**: Both light and dark mode, system-aware with manual toggle. Light mode is the default (client-demo-friendly); dark mode for power users.
- **Typography**: High-contrast, clean sans-serif. Strong hierarchy between headings and body. Labels and metadata in muted tones.
- **Motion**: Purposeful and restrained — Framer Motion transitions on page changes, subtle skeleton loaders during AI generation, progress indicators that feel alive. No gratuitous animation.
- **Anti-references**: Avoid neon-gamer aesthetics, heavy gradients, overly bold/athletic styling. This is a *tool for creating* sports content, not a sports brand itself.
- **Soft radii**: `rounded-xl` on cards, `rounded-lg` on inputs/buttons — approachable but not playful.

### Design Principles
1. **Clarity over cleverness** — Every element earns its place. No decorative complexity. If it doesn't aid comprehension or action, remove it.
2. **Trust through polish** — Subtle details (consistent spacing, refined shadows, pixel-perfect alignment) signal that the AI underneath is reliable and production-grade.
3. **Progressive disclosure** — Complex multi-step workflows (persona → script → video) should feel simple at each step. Hide depth until needed.
4. **Status is sacred** — AI jobs take time. Generation states, progress, errors, and completions must always be visible, honest, and never anxiety-inducing.
5. **Scale-first layout** — Batch operations, multi-client views, and data tables are first-class citizens. Design for 10 projects, not just one.

### Technical Constraints
- Stack: Next.js (App Router), React, TailwindCSS, shadcn/ui, Framer Motion
- shadcn/ui components should be customized to match the indigo/violet palette, not used out-of-box
- Tailwind config should define a custom color ramp for the primary (indigo/violet), neutral (slate), and semantic (success, warning, error) scales
- 9:16 video preview components are a key UI primitive — plan for vertical aspect-ratio containers throughout
